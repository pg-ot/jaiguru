Common Questions

Frequently Asked

Is this program suitable for beginners? +

Yes. The 21-Day Reset is designed for all levels. Guru provides personalized modifications based on your current health status and experience level.

Do I need special equipment? +

Minimal equipment needed. A yoga mat and comfortable clothing are sufficient. All practices can be done at home.

Can I follow this program with medical conditions? +

Always consult your healthcare provider first. Our program complements medical treatment but does not replace it. Inform Guru of any conditions during enrollment.

What if I miss a day during the 21-day program? +

Consistency is key, but life happens. Simply resume the next day. Guru provides guidance on maintaining momentum even with occasional interruptions.

Are sessions conducted online or in-person? +

Both options available. Online sessions via video call for global participants, and in-person sessions at our Tamil Nadu location by appointment.

What dietary restrictions are required? +

We focus on conscious eating rather than strict restrictions. Guru provides personalized food guidelines based on your constitution, goals, and lifestyle.

What happens after the 21 days? +

You receive lifetime access to program materials and ongoing community support. Many participants continue with monthly mentorship sessions to deepen their practice.
